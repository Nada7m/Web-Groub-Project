Welcome to SOLAND!

Where we turn dreamy travel fantasies into reality! We are a team of passionate individuals who came together to create an innovative platform for flight bookings, trips, and tourism.

Our name SOLAND is inspired by the Latin word “Sol” meaning sun, and “Land” meaning earth — reflecting our vision of connecting every corner of this planet for our valued clients. The name also carries a deeply personal touch,as it’s linked to the initials of our names, symbolizing our unity and identity.

Our Vision

To make travel experiences brighter and more surreal — just like the sunrise of a new day.

Our Mission

To provide an innovative platform that makes it easy and comfortable for travelers to book their journeys, offering comprehensive options and exceptional customer service. At SOLAND, we strive to be your gateway to new worlds.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Delivering new ideas to redefine the travel experience.
  • Teamwork: Five minds, one goal — your comfort.
  • Inspired Sustainability: Because the sun and the earth are our eternal gifts, and we must protect them with love and responsibility.
